Not Like Us

Song Title

Not Like Us

Performer

Kendrick Lamar

My Review

Kendrick absolutely killed it with this song. He has always been a lyrical genius, but this song is on another level. Not only did Kendrick destroy Drake, he did so in an upbeat, catchy song that appeals to all listeners, even those who may not usually like rap. This song can be blasted in the car or the club. In my opinion, this song should be higher on the list. I give it 5 out of 5 stars.
