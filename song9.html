Beautiful Things

Song Title

Beautiful Things

Performer

Benson Boone

My Review

Similar to Teddy Swims, I hadn’t heard of Benson Boone before this song. He has a strong voice and you can hear the emotion behind it. While his type of music isn’t my personal favorite, I can see why so many people like it. I know this song also went viral on TikTok which I’m sure helped it land on the Billboard charts. Overall, I think it’s good but too intense to blast while I’m trying to relax on a summer day. I rate it 3/5 stars.
